Orchidaceae Cytogenetic Counts Database

Orchidaceae is one of the most diverse groups of flowering plants, in number of species, geographic distribution, and in cytogenetic characters. This website aims to provide the collection of data on chromosome number (haploid and/or diploid), genome size and additional chromosomal markers resulted from the project developed by the Laboratory of Evolution and Cytogenomics of Plants at Federal University of ABC, Brazil.
